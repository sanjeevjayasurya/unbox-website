# Unbox Robotics — LLM Content Guide

## About
Unbox Robotics (UnboxRobotics Labs Pvt Ltd) is a warehouse automation company founded in 2019, headquartered in Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra, India. We design and deploy intelligent robotic systems for parcel sortation and order consolidation using swarm robotics.

## Core Product
**UnboxSort** — a modular, vertical robotic parcel sortation system that uses autonomous swarm robots to sort parcels into 2000+ destinations with 99.9%+ accuracy. It saves 50–70% floor space vs. traditional conveyors and can be deployed in 6–9 weeks.
